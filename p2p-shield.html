How to Play

The Goal

Calculate an answer as close to the Target as possible using your 3 cards and any math operators.

Phase 1: Trick Phase

Cards are face-down. Use this time to prepare:

  • Play Tricks: Use pre-round (*) cards to mess with the target or set scoring rules.
  • Scout (1 🔄): Spend a Redraw to see your own cards early.
  • Spy (1 🔄): Spend a Redraw to see an opponent's cards.

Phase 2: Solve Phase

Cards are revealed! Work fast to submit:

  • Redraw: Spend remaining tokens to get new cards.
  • Play Tricks: Use during-round (!) cards anytime.
  • Submit: Enter your final answer. Fastest solver with points gets a +1 speed bonus!

Defensive Play: The Shield 🛡️

If you hold a Goblin Shield, you'll get a 3-second window to Deflect offensive tricks played by opponents. Act fast to save the round!
